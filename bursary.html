BURSARY

REQUREMENTS FOR UNIVERSITIES, COLLEGES AND SPECIAL SCHOOLS:-

  1. School Identification card Or National Identification card.
  2. The Fees structures / statement.
  3. The Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education slip.
  4. The Admission Letter to join the Institution.

REQUIREMENTS FOR SECONDARY SCHOOLS

  1. School Identification Card.
  2. The Kenya Certificate of Primary Education slip.
  3. The Fees Structures / statement.
  4. The Admission Letter to join the Institution.

PLEASE NOTE:-

  • Application forms from previous years will not be considered
  • Incomplete applications will not be considered. Follow directions on the form carefully
  • Applications filled out by anyone other than the requesting student will not be considered
  • All application forms have a serial number associated with the applicant it was given or emailed to. Any forms that are returned to us where the serial number does not match the student to whom it was assigned will not be considered
  • Application forms are not a guarantee of scholarship
  • Scanning of the original and photocopy is required.
